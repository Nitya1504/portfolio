TERAHEAL

AI-Powered Wellness Platform

Personalized health predictions through genetic and lifestyle analysis

Project Overview

TERAHEAL is an innovative AI-driven wellness platform that combines genetic analysis with lifestyle data to predict hereditary health risks and provide personalized wellness recommendations. Using cutting-edge machine learning models, it offers early health risk detection and adaptive wellness planning.

Technologies Used

CNNs RNNs LSTMs Transformers Autoencoders GNNs React Streamlit Reinforcement Learning

Key Features

Genetic Analysis

Advanced AI models analyze genetic data to identify hereditary health risk patterns.

Lifestyle Integration

Combines genetic data with lifestyle factors for comprehensive health assessment.

Personalized Plans

Creates adaptive wellness plans using reinforcement learning and epigenetic modeling.

Interactive Dashboard

Real-time visualization of genetic reports and wellness insights through responsive interface.

AI Architecture

  • CNNs: Process genetic sequence data and identify patterns in genomic information
  • Transformers: Handle complex relationships between genetic variants and health outcomes
  • Autoencoders: Dimensionality reduction for genetic data and feature extraction
  • GNNs: Model genetic networks and gene interaction pathways
  • Reinforcement Learning: Optimize personalized wellness recommendations
